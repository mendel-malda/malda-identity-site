About

Malda provides Identity and Access Management advisory and architecture support for teams building secure authentication and authorization. The focus is pragmatic: reduce risk, improve reliability, and keep costs sensible.

How I work

  • Start with goals, constraints, and threat model.
  • Review current architecture, flows, and operational reality.
  • Deliver clear recommendations: what to change now vs later.
  • Provide a practical reference architecture and standards.

Typical outcomes

  • Cleaner OAuth2/OIDC flows and fewer edge-case auth bugs.
  • Better session security, MFA posture, and recovery strategy.
  • Aligned teams: shared standards, clearer responsibilities.
  • Reduced delivery risk through early architectural validation.
